I watched this movie with no thought and did not expect much. This movie is leaps ahead of my expectation. Another anime movie that just blew me away by being so simple yet messy. The time travel concept was easy to understand, thank you for that. I'm not used to seeing this stuff easy.

<!--more-->

What is with these Japanese Anime movies that they get me feel emotional somehow. Initially, the characters in this movie seemed less connected and it was all about the protagonist. But I was wrong. The relationship between the three friends and them being together for the most part was an important element in the plot.

Now coming to the time travel stuff. Not much details about the process was given and things were kept ambiguous. I'm glad that they did not focus more on this. Makoto kept going back and forth in time. The movie revolves around the impact that this has on her and people around her.

Three friends have a simple life until the protagonist Makoto finds a way to leap time. At first, she is not sure about her ability. But when she finds out about this, she keeps going back and correct her mistakes. In that process, she creates more problems for others. Every time she saves herself from a bad thing, someone else faces it. She was not aware about this, but soon she realizes this as the ones close to her start to get impacted.

There are multiple characters and they have feelings about each other. Makoto tries to escape from confessing to someone's feelings. Later she feels that it was not a good idea. She helps people who are confessing love and rather strangely feels bad about it. This involves a lot of time leap as she creates more problems than she solves. Eventually she is successful. Things are good.

One of her friend turns out to be a time traveller. Chiaki had a device that enables time leap. He lost it and that ended up with Makoto. The number of times you can leap is not unlimited. Now, Makoto has used up her leaps and Chiaki also somehow used up the last chance to save a friend. Now he has no way to go back to future.

Chiaki explains his reason to travel back to this time. He wanted to see a painting which was destroyed in the future. But in this time, that painting is up for restoration. Thus he can't see it still. He just leaves Makoto and explains that he can't see her again.

Somehow they figure out a way to get one more chance for time leap. Makoto gets back to they all of this started. She then tells Chiaki to go back. Every problem she solved is back to where it was. Now she has to deal with it. But she is happy because she got the lesson that she can't escape her bad luck.

She promised Chiaki that he will see the painting in the future as she will do everything that she could to preserve it. She was also looking for a career path and I think this implies that she will take a course related to restoration. Moreover, the lady who was in the restoration work has a photo which I thought had some reference to Chiaki. But I think that's not what it was.

I like movies which show loss that is not usual. In this case, Chiaki left forever and there is no way to find out that they will meet in the future. The way the movie is spun together is amazing. All the incidents go through all of their possiblities and in the end come back to the initial case. A unique view of the consequences is shown. Makoto is handling the issues in a more matured way and that shows how she grew with time. The themes were completely new to me and that also kind of stood out. There are just too many movies with time travel and I thought this could be one of them but it was not.

I will not forget this movie for a long time. Don't know how people think stories like these. Would want to write something like this. Simple stories and varied impact. No high-level sci-fi stuff. Just some characters with feelings that and a conscious person trying to control the time.
