## Reliablity

- It means roughly, "continuing to work correctly, even when things go wrong."
- Fault is one component of system deviating from its spec and failure is when the whole system stops providing the required service to the user.
- Impossible to get faults down to zero. Best to design mechanisms that prevent faults from causing failures.
